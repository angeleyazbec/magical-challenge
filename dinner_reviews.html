    Magical Main Courses


    Curious how Princess Armelle and her Royal Courtier Judges evaluated her meals and the chefs who prepared them? See their reviews below!

    Yak and Yeti Meal:
    Marinated steak and corn. Red pepper, snap peas, mushroom, and hot pepper spice, with a side of baby carrots.

    Princess Armelle absolutely loved her meal! Chef Jose was very creative with marinating her steak and adding some zingy spices to her vegetables, which added exquisite flavor to Princess Armelle's meal. Her family simply could not feed her fast enough. She cleared all of her dishes and was even reaching into her empty dishes in the hopes of enjoying an additional bite. Chef Jose really set the bar with this meal. All other chefs will have to rise to the challenge.
    Teppan Edo Meal:
    Chicken with a blend of vegetables.

    Princess Armelle would like to give an honorable mention to the Sous Chef at Teppan Edo who prepared her dinner. She enjoyed her food and finished it all. As always, the show by the hibachi-trained chef was amusing and entertaining to her and her family.
    Olivia's Cafe Meal:
    Beef with rosemary, garlic, and olive oil. Green beans and sweet potatoes.

    Princess Armelle was absolutely delighted with her meal! She was very hungry prior to her meal being served and devoured every bite once it arrived. Chef Jeremy was extremely kind and prepared a delectable meal fit for the Princess. He even created a special presentation plate that brought a smile to Princess Armelle's face. Chef Jeremy also went above and beyond in his service to the Princess by making her extra sweet potatoes to enjoy the next day. Chef Jeremy rose to the challenge and went above and beyond in his service.
    Le Creperie Meal:
    Chicken, pears, apples, and peaches.

    Princess Armelle would like to give an honorable mention to the fine chef at Le Creperie who was kind enough to accommodate her dietary needs. While Princess Armelle could not enjoy a crepe, the chef prepared her a unique crepe filling of her own. A blend of chicken, apples, pears, and peaches was the perfect meal to serve to the Princess. She enjoyed her refreshing meal and gives her compliments to the chef.
    Space 220 Meal:
    Chicken, potatoes, carrots, and zucchini.

    Princess Armelle had a lunch that was out of this world! Chef Jorge's presentation was top-notch, and the Princess could not wait to eat her food. Chef Jorge was very attentive to Princess Armelle's needs and double checked which vegetables she could have prior to preparing her meal. Her meal was served at the perfect temperature and there were no leftovers to be found. Chef Jorge ensured this competition is going to be tough with an out of this world presentation and delicious food.
    Liberty Tree Meal:
    Turkey, green beans, and mashed potatoes with gravy.

    Princess Armelle thoroughly enjoyed her meal and devoured not one but two whole portions of this magical dish! Chef Al paid meticulous attention to detail as he triple checked all ingredients to ensure they met Princess Armelle's dietary needs and verified the proper consistency of her food prior to serving. This meal was so delicious that the Princess pouted when her dish was taken away for her second helping. Chef Al was very kind, extremely detail-oriented, and most deserving of Princess Armelle's high honours. Other chefs are going to have a hard time meeting the high quality service of Chef Al.
    Roundup Rodeo BBQ Meal:
    Brisket, corn nibblets, zucchini, and peppers.

    Princess Armelle enjoyed her barbeque feast, prepared by Chef Carlos. Chef Carlos was very attentive to her needs and double checked the ingredients the Princess was allowed to eat. Princess Armelle was pleased with the good tasting food and cleared all of her plates. Chef Carlos prepared a barbeque feast worthy of celebrating Andy coming home from Comboy Camp.
    Mama Melrose's Meal:
    Baked chicken, green beans with basil, and carrots.

    Princess Armelle enjoyed her meal prepared by Chef John. She was quite hungry before her meal and cleaned her plate. Chef John was accommodating, kind, and very quick with his service. The Princess' meal arrived soon after her order was placed. Princess Armelle appreciated the quick service and delicious meal. Chef John prepared a magical meal fit for a princess and was accommodating to Her Royal Highness.
