    Princess Armelle's Magical Plate Challenge 2023




    Hear ye, hear ye!

    Princess Armelle of the Kingdom of Light has come for her Annual Royal Tour of Walt Disney World Resort in Florida. Every year since she was a baby, Princess Armelle has visited Disney World. She enjoys the whole magical experience: the rides, the shows, the music, the fireworks, the lights on her chariot, and the food, most of all. There is hardly anything she enjoys more than eating a particularly magical meal that satisfies her voracious appetite and makes her feel like the royal princess she is. A close second would be the lights her royal chariot, which are exquistely and expertly designed by her royal Mechatronics Engineer.

    It is not easy, however, to please Princess Armelle when it comes to her food. While she is a princess of few words, she will let you know how much she likes, or dislikes, her food. To please Princess Armelle, the food must be pureed to a perfect consistency with the right complementary blend of proteins, potatoes, vegetables, and spices that she simply cannot resist. Also, just like every other princess, do not let her size fool you: she can eat her weight in food. Thus, an adequate portion size is also very important to Princess Armelle. She simply cannot leave from a meal hungry and dissatisfied with how her food was prepared.


    Therefore, Princess Armelle's royal family has challenged the chefs at Walt Disney World Resort to create the most magical meal for Princess Armelle. Each dinner and dessert will be judged by the following categories:


      Portion Size
      Presentation
      Taste
      Temperature
      Texture

    Of course, it is not enough to simply create an amazing dinner and dessert for Princess Armelle, but the chef himself or herself must also provide a magical experience for the princess. Therefore, the chefs themselves will also be rated on the following categories:

      Kindness
      Accommodating
      Attentiveness to Needs
      Timeliness

    The restaurants of Princess Armelle's 2023 Annual Royal Tour are posted on this site. You can see the featured restaurants, magical dinners and desserts prepared for the princess, as well as her review, and her Royal Taster's review, of each dish. The winner will be announced after the conclusion of Princess Armelle's tour.
