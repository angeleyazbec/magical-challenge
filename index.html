    Hear ye, hear ye!
    Princess Armelle's personal Walt Disney World Master Royal Chef Competition 2023 is here!

    Princess Armelle of the Kingdom of Magical Lights has come for her Annual Royal Tour of Walt Disney World Resort in Florida. Every year since she was a baby, Princess Armelle has visited Disney World. She enjoys the whole magical experience: the rides, the shows, the music, the fireworks, the lights on her chariot, and the food, most of all. There is hardly anything she enjoys more than eating a particularly magical meal that satisfies her voracious appetite and makes her feel like the royal princess she is. A close second would be the lights her royal chariot, which are exquisitely and expertly designed by her royal Mechatronics Engineer.

    It is not easy, however, to please Princess Armelle when it comes to her food. While she is a princess of few words, she will let you know how much she likes, or dislikes, her food. To please Princess Armelle, the food must be pureed to a perfect consistency with the right complementary blend of meat, potatoes, vegetables, and spices that she simply cannot resist. Also, just like every other princess, do not let her size fool you: she can eat her weight in food. Thus, an adequate portion size is also very important to Princess Armelle. She simply cannot leave from a meal hungry and dissatisfied with how her food was prepared.

    Only the finest chefs will be admitted into
    Her Royal Highness' Order of Magical Culinary Ears,
    and thus bestowed the high honour of
    Knight/Dame of Princess Armelle's Royal Chefs' Round Table.

    Each main course and dessert will be judged by the following categories:

      Texture
      Temperature
      Taste
      Portion Size
      Presentation

    Of course, it is not enough to simply create a magical main course and dessert for Princess Armelle, but the chef himself or herself must also provide a magical experience for the princess. Therefore, the chefs will also be rated on the following categories:

      Understanding of Needs
      Accommodating
      Desire to Please
      Kindness

    The restaurants of Princess Armelle's 2023 Annual Royal Tour are posted on this site. You can see the featured restaurants, magical main courses and desserts prepared for the princess, and her review, as well as her Royal Courtier Judges' review, of each msin course and dessert. Each main course and dessert will have its review posted by 9 am the morning after Princess Armelle's meal.
    The winner will be announced after the conclusion of Princess Armelle's tour.
