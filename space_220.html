    Space 220 at EPCOT





    Space 220 offers a unique, themed dining experience within EPCOT's expanded Mission: SPACE Pavilion. Advertised as having an "out-of-this-world" dining experience, their menu offers space-themed lift-offs (appetizers) and star courses (main dishes). Space 220's featured lift-offs include their Blue Moon Cauliflower and Space Greens. Featured star courses include the Galactic Salmon and the Gemini Burger. Space 220's menu certainly piques the interest of the adventurous diner seeking a high-class dining experience in the stars.

    Space 220 is a new stop Princess Armelle's family decided to add to this year's tour. They look forward to a stellar new adventure and experience with great anticipation. Whether Space 220 will be added as a regular stop on future tours will largely depend on Princess Armelle's dining experience there.
