    Olivia's Cafe





    Olivia's Cafe features home-styled cooking with a taste of the Florida Keys. Located at Disney's Old Key West Resort, Olivia's Cafe is a very convenient dining location for Princess Armelle and her family. Both the Brunch and Dinner menus offer selections that are reminiscent of American comfort food and a splash of Southern cooking. Top brunch menu items include Banana Bread Pudding, Southern Buttermilk Chicken, and the famous Olivia's Breakfast. Dinner options include Prime Rib, Shrimp Pasta, and Bacon Cheeseburger.

    Olivia's Cafe is a newer favorite of Princess Armelle's family. The taste of home and high-quality service are top features of this restaurant. Banana Bread Pudding is a personal favorite of the family, but just about all dishes on the menu offer a delightful dining experience. Whether you are looking for a casual brunch or a hearty dinner, Olivia's Cafe will suit your dining needs.
