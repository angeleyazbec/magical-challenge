    Magical Desserts


    Everyone knows a good dessert is the perfect complement to a delicious dinner. Curious how Princess Armelle and her Royal Courtier Judges evaluated her desserts and the chefs who prepared them? See their reviews below!

    Yak and Yeti Dessert:
    Kiwi Melon Sorbet.

    Kudos to Chef Jose for making Princess Armelle a custom dessert! Chef used his ingenuity and creativity to make a completely off-menu dessert that thoroughly pleased Princess Armelle. Her only regret is that she was so full from dinner that she had to take home the rest of her dessert. Chef Jose was very kind and provided insulated takeout containers to help transport her sorbet back home. Princess Armelle loves sorbet, but this Kiwi Melon Sorbet holds a special place in her heart... or stomach, as we should say.

    Liberty Tree Dessert:
    Raspberry Sorbet.

    Chef Al served a rich raspberry sorbet for Princess Armelle's dessert. Raspberry sorbet is the Princess' favorite dessert, but this sorbet had the most authentic flavor of real raspberries she had ever had in a sorbet. Kudos to Chef Al for having such an exquisite and flavorful dessert available to serve Princess Armelle.
