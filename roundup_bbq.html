    Roundup Rodeo BBQ at Hollywood Studios






    Roundup Rodeo BBQ is a brand-new restaurant at Hollywood Studios, opening in Spring 2023. This restaurant is western-themed and features Andy's lovable toys. According to Disney's website, you will see the world through the eyes of Andy's toys. That should surely complete the experience of being in Hollywood Studios' Toy Story Land! The menu showcases an all-you-can-enjoy barbeque feast. The main highlights include cheddar biscuits with sweet pepper jelly, a variety of salads, barbequed meats, macaroni and cheese, loaded potato barrels, roasted vegetables, fried pickles, and more! You also get to choose from a selection of desserts, which include classic apple pie and chocolate silk pie.

    Princess Armelle and her family are delighted that a new restaurant has opened at Hollywood Studios, especially one with such a variety of food options and a playful atmosphere. They are quite eager to enjoy one of the newest dining experiences at Walt Disney World, and determine whether Roundup Rodeo BBQ becomes a regular stop on future royal tours.
