    Teppan Edo at the World Showcase in EPCOT




    Teppan Edo provides a hibachi steakhouse dining experience for all of its guests. Highly skilled chefs entertain you with a quintessential dinner and a show, featuring Teppan-yaki style cooking. Your typical meal will a small salad, protein of your choice, stir-fried vegetables, and rice. Teppan Edo also provides a selection of unique desserts, some of which have the unique green tea flavor associated with Japanese culture.

    A meal at Teppan Edo is sure to satisfy the taste buds of the pickiest of eaters as well as satiate the palettes of enthusiatic foodies. Princess Armelle's family rates this restaurant of having one of the best dining shows Walt Disney World Resort has to offer.
