    Liberty Tree Tavern at Magical Kingdom





    Liberty Tree Tavern at Magical Kingdom takes you back to the colonial times of the United States, with servers, hosts, and hostesses dressed in period clothing. The interior is reminiscent of a colonial-style inn and the meal is one of the best Thanksgiving-style meals in the country. You start your meal with a refreshing Declaration Salad, followed by a bountiful Patriot's Platter, complete with turkey, roast beef, pot roast, mashed potatoes, stuffing, and vegetables. Warm and comforting toffee cake with vanilla ice cream is your dessert to top off the feast.

    Liberty Tree Tavern is a personal favorite of Princess Armelle's family, especially her beloved father. Her mother greatly appreciates that someone else is putting in the hard work of cooking the iconic all-American meal. The food is always delicious and diners are sure to leave with turkey-induced comas with the tavern's all-you-care-to-enjoy servings.
