    Yak & Yeti Restaurant at Animal Kingdom





    Yak & Yeti Restaurant at Animal Kingdom provides a wide variety of delicious pan-Asian foods that will not disappoint. This restaurant offers some typical Asian appetizers, such as Wonton Soup and Pot Stickers. They also offer so many different main courses, you will surely want to visit again so you can enjoy them all! They offer dishes familiar to the American palette, such as Sweet & Sour Chicken, Lo Mein, and Honey Chicken, with stir-fried veggies and rice. They also offer more "exotic" dishes such as their signature Chicken Tikka Masala, Korean Beef, and Bhaktapur Duck. If you have room for dessert, you can also enjoy delicious dessert options as such Fried Wontons and Mango Key Lime Cheesecake.

    Princess Armelle's family rates Yak & Yeti Restaurant as having the widest selection of dishes. In fact, it is the only restaurant the family consistently visits twice during their annual tour. With dishes to please the whole family and so many options to choose from, there is simply no way one's curiosity could be satisfied with just one visit. If you are looking for a restaurant that delivers consistent quality and variety, look no further than Yak & Yeti.
