    Winners 2023



    Princess Armelle's personal
    Walt Disney World
    Master Royal Chef Competition 2023



    Hear ye, hear ye!
    Princess Armelle's has announced the winners of her personal
    Master Royal Chef Competition 2023.

    But first, it should be noted that
    this competition was really tight among the chefs.
    A mere two tenths of a point separated first and second place.
    Just one point separated second and third place.

    Princess Armelle and her family formally express their gratitude to all
    of the chefs for preparing such wonderful meals
    and being so kind to the Princess.


    The winners are hereby decreed on this 10th day of May, 2023.

    Master Royal Chef:
    Chef Jose of Yak & Yeti Restaurant
    at Animal Kingdom





    Co-Second Royal Chef:
    Chef Jeremy of Olivia's Cafe
    at Old Key West Resort




    Co-Second Royal Chef:
    Chef Michael of Olivia's Cafe
    at Old Key West Resort




    Star Royal Chef:
    Chef Al of Liberty Tree Tavern
    at Magic Kingdom

    These honorable chefs will be presented with ribbons and certificates

    Princess Armelle formally thanks each and every chef in her competition for preparing magical dishes and providing high quality service. She is sad that she cannot hire the Disney chefs as her personal, full-time chefs, but looks forward to returning to
    the Kingdom of Mouse for her next Annual Royal Tour.
