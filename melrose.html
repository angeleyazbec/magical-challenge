    Mama Melrose's Restaurant Italiano





    Mama Melrose's provides a laid-back dining atmosphere, which is perfect respite of a long day at the park. The American Italian dishes are sure to satisfy any diner looking for a comforting meal. In true Italian style, you can expect to take your time at your meal with time for a leisurely discourse over warm bread and olive oil and spice mix, followed by a healthy sized dinner that will surely satisfy you for the rest of the night. Of course, if you have room for the dessert portion of your stomach, you can indulge in Italian-style desserts, such as gelato, cannoli, and tiramisu.

    Mama Melrose's is the royal family's personal favorite restaurant at Hollywood Studios. Over their many tours, this restaurant remains on the list of family favorite places to eat, and that says lot, given the number of tours they have taken! The family's favorite dish is the Chicken Parmesan, which is always made to perfection and comes complete with a side of spaghetti, marinara sauce, and mozarella cheese.
